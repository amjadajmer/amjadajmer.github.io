Amjad Khan is a salesforce consultant specializing in bespoke, artisanal, single origin, grass fed, fixed gear, and slow roasted apps & websites for clients of all sizes. Currently a Principal UX Designer at projekt202, Amjad is capable within a wide range of tactical and big-picture design tasks.

A little bit more about Amjad. Like anyone cares.

Amjad is passionate about making sure that systems, interfaces, language and graphics are human-friendly, emotive, aesthetically pleasing, clear, on-brand and usable — with a touch of mellow smoothness. He has worked in a variety of environments, from mega-consultancies to small-ish agencies to product companies. From workflow diagrams to detailed user interface mockups, Amjad loves shaping and crafting on-screen experiences.

When Amjad is not being a jerk by forgetting to label his Photoshop layers, eating all the office candy, and showing up for work on time every day (so annoying), he enjoys never being satisfied with his own work, sci-fi, music, photography, movies, toast, and throwing down in fisticuffs with CSS.
